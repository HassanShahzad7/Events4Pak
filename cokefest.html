Coke Fest



What is Coke Fest?

The most awaited festival of the year is back! This time, CokeFest is Powered by Jazz Super 4G starting from the 16th of June. It is a festival, which is an attraction for people of all ages in multiple cities of Pakistan. An amazing recreational event for the whole family featuring numerous artists, activities, food stalls and much more. Jazz is not only a partner in making the event more exciting but is also offering some amazing offers, promotions, and FREE WiFi at the venue!
